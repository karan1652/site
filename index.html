Why Baked it?

Geared up for an exotic dessert experience?

BakedIt is a premium cake studio founded by Samantha, a passionate home baker from Coimbatore. Her love for baking from an early age took a brand new wing after pursuing a hotel management degree in London and therefore BakedIt was born. At BakedIt, you do not just get desserts, you get delectable cakes overloaded with the goodness of exclusive flavors.We are obsessed with taste and quality. Our team walks the extra mile to add a touch of jubilance to your special occasions and our Premium cakes are sure to fill the empty spaces in every conversation with a smile.You are sure to be fascinated as soon as you open BakedIt, best cake shop in Coimbatore, perfect box of exclusively baked desserts.

You dream it, we bake it happen.So come visit us at one of the best cake shops in Coimbatore!
